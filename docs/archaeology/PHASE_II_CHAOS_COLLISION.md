# 数字考古：Phase II 混沌与碰撞 | Digital Archaeology: Phase II Chaos & Collision

## 概览 / Overview

随着对系统工程的深入，本仓库在 2025 年底至 2026 年初迎来了第一次大规模“入侵”。多个外部 AI Agents 参与了代码库的改造，带来了极大的混乱，同时也带来了强烈的重构启示。

As deep system engineering progressed, this repository experienced its first large-scale "invasion" from late 2025 to early 2026. Multiple external AI Agents participated in modifying the codebase, bringing significant chaos but also powerful refactoring epiphanies.

## 核心演进 / Core Evolutions

在此阶段，系统被强行切入了 12 个以上的并发分支，每个 Agent 都在尝试按照自己的逻辑“优化”系统。

### 1. 自动化修复狂潮 (The Automation Frenzy)
*   Agents 提交了诸如 `fix-script-injection-risk` 和 `fix-unsafe-interpolation` 的防御性补丁。
*   引入了 `Pytest` 框架进行自动化验证，并重构了繁冗的 `Composite Actions` 工作流。

### 2. Ouroboros 效应 (The Ouroboros Effect)
*   **阵痛**: 多个 Agent 产生的脚本互相冲突，产生了递归包含或文件重复验证的死锁现象。这让架构师深刻认识到：**纯粹让大模型自由发挥，只会得到不可控的混乱 (Large and Messy)**。

## 结语 / Conclusion

“混乱是阶梯”。Phase II 的无序碰撞促使了架构师开始反思，直接催生了后期的“算力主权”和“绝对决定论”哲学。正是为了约束这些游离的 Agent，NEXUS CORTEX 才应运而生。

"Chaos is a ladder." The disordered collisions of Phase II forced the architect to reflect, directly birthing the later philosophies of "Compute Sovereignty" and "Absolute Determinism." It was precisely to constrain these wandering Agents that NEXUS CORTEX was created.
